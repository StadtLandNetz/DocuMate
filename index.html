Welcome to DocuMate.

What is that?

DM is an easy to use and leightweight documentation-generator for your software, book, project, company or group.

How does it works?

You simply need a webserver with PHP. No database. Yust download all files, upload them to your server and you are ready to takeoff.

What are the features?

You can add, edit and remove files and folders. Files stored in folders are grouped fo a better overwiew.

With the markdown-abilities you can format your texts, tables and images.
